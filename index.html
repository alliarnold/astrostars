Twinkling Personalities

Astrology has become increasing popular in American internet culture, with more and more essays coming out explaining your sign. Learn about which words and sentiments are most frequently apparent in descriptions of the twelve zodiac signs based on a sample of American web publications.

Are you an emotional Cancer? Or a passionate Aries? See which signs scored highest for each sentiment.

Sources

Sentiment and key word analysis was done on a sample of zodiac descriptions published online by: Allure, Astrology.com, Co-star, Elle, The Framer's Almanac, Glamour, NY Mag, NY Post, OK Player, Parade, Purewow, Refinery29, Shape, USA Today, Vice, Women's Health, and ZodiacAstrology.com.
